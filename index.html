Our Mission

The Brownville Fine Arts Association strives to promote the visual and performing arts and pledges to weave them into Brownville's historically and culturally rich setting. We also recognize the gratifying beauty in nature and the outdoors. We endeavor to maintain a place for those who want to develop the creative process, expand aesthetic appreciation, and derive new meaning and understanding from the arts and nature.

About Brownville Fine Arts Association

As a non-profit member organization, the Brownville Fine Arts Association serves as a vital volunteer resources that supports cultural, educational and recreational events and activities within Brownville. Examples of these endeavors include the Schoolhouse Art Gallery & Nature Center, Gov. Furnas House Museum, and Gov. Robert W. Furnas Arboretum & Whiskey Run Creek Nature Trail.
